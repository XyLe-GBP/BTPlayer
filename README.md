# BTPlayer - BlueTooth audio Player 
Play audio via Bluetooth A2DP Sink(SNK) on Windows.

## What is this?
This is a simple application that uses Windows A2DP to connect Bluetooth-enabled devices, such as smartphones and portable audio players (DAPs), to your PC, allowing you to play audio through your PC.

## Instructions for Use
**Your PC must have Bluetooth capability.**
**(If your PC does not have this capability, this application will not work!**
**In that case, please purchase and install a Wi-Fi LAN card or similar device, install a Bluetooth receiver, or replace your motherboard.)**

- Devices Capable of Bluetooth Playback

If you are using the portable EXE version, you will need the .NET 10 runtime.

### example usage

There are various ways to use this app. For example, in games where you can’t freely change the music (such as racing games),
you can set the in-game music to 0 and leave only the sound effects on, then use this app to play your favorite music while you play.

Also, if you find that your smartphone’s speakers aren’t loud enough when watching videos on services like YouTube, you can play the audio at a higher volume through your computer.
How you use it is up to you :)
